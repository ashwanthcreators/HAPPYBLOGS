College Life is one of the most remarkable and lovable times of an individual's life. Unlike School Life,
College Life has a different experience, and a person needs to have this experience in his/her life.
College Life exposes us to whole new experiences which we always dream of experiencing after our school life.
For every person, College Life has a different meaning. While some people spend their college life by partying with friends,
others become more cautious about their career and study hard.
Whatever the way, every individual enjoy their college life and always wish to relive that time once it is over.
